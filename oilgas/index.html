Notes

Bar height is symlog-scaled. Only counties with non-zero production are shown. Scroll to zoom, drag to pan. Hover a county to see exact values. Gas is measured in MCF (thousands of cubic feet).

Data Sources

Production values for 2000–2011 are drawn from the USDA ERS county-level dataset, which covers onshore production in the lower 48 states. This source captures approximately 60% of EIA-reported national totals; offshore production is excluded by design. Values for 1970–1999 and 2012–2020 are spatially aggregated from the USGS well-history dataset and match approximately 95–99% of EIA-reported national totals. A discontinuity at the 2011–2012 boundary reflects the difference in source coverage, not an actual step change in production. Pre-1970 USGS data was excluded due to poor digitization coverage.
