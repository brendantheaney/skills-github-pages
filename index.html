About Me

Hello! I'm Brendan Heaney, a Sophomore at Binghamton majoring in Economics and Mathematics. I made this website, mainly, because when tutoring I found some ways of explaining statistics concepts seemed to be a bit simpler than others, or were easier for me to explain, or matched what people were taught better, but I wanted to have to have a list of methods in one place for my own use in tutoring. Scrolling through bookmarks was a bit unweildy, especially when I needed to use some of the same concepts quite often.

What I upload here is designed for the Math 147 courseload at Binghamton, although I hope it has some applicability outside of that. I may also upload some content for Math 447 and 448 here and there, mainly for my own use. If you have any questions feel free to contact me, preferably at my Binghamton Email address.
