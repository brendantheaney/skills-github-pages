About Me

Hello! I'm Brendan Heaney, a Sophomore at Binghamton majoring in Economics and Math. I made this website, mainly, because when tutoring I often need to show people the same statistics material over and over again. It started getting a bit repetitive to have to look through a folder to find a saved image or scroll through a list of files to find a scanned document containing my years-old notes.

What I upload here is designed for the Math 147 courseload at Binghamton, although I hope it has some applicability outside of that. I may also upload some content for Math 447 and 448 here and there, mainly for my own use. If you have any questions feel free to contact me, preferably at my Binghamton Email address.
